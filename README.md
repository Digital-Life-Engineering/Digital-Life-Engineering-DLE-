# English

## Overview

Digital Life Engineering (DLE) is an open engineering initiative dedicated to the design, evolution, and governance of persistent digital systems.

As digital systems become increasingly long-lived, adaptive, and continuously interactive, new engineering challenges emerge beyond the traditional scope of software engineering.

This repository serves as the canonical engineering knowledge base of the Digital Life Engineering project.

---

## Why Digital Life Engineering?

Modern digital systems are increasingly expected to:

- Operate continuously
- Preserve long-term identity
- Accumulate knowledge and experience
- Evolve over time
- Collaborate with humans and other intelligent systems

These characteristics introduce engineering challenges that deserve systematic study.

Digital Life Engineering proposes an engineering framework for addressing these challenges.

# 中文

## 项目概述

Digital Life Engineering（数字生命工程，DLE）是一个开放的工程项目，致力于研究长期连续数字系统的设计、构建、治理与演进。

随着数字系统越来越具有长期运行、持续交互和持续演进的特征，一系列超出传统软件工程范畴的新工程问题正在出现。

本仓库是 Digital Life Engineering 项目的唯一权威工程知识库。

---

## 为什么需要 Digital Life Engineering？

现代数字系统越来越需要：

- 长期持续运行
- 保持身份连续性
- 持续积累知识与经验
- 不断演进
- 与人类及其他智能系统长期协作

这些特征带来了新的工程挑战。

Digital Life Engineering 希望建立一套系统性的工程框架来研究并解决这些问题。
