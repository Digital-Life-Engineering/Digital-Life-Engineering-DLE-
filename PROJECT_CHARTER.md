# Project Charter
# 项目宪章

## English

### Purpose

The purpose of the Digital Life Engineering (DLE) project is to establish an open engineering foundation for the design, construction, governance, and evolution of persistent digital systems.

### Mission

To establish an open, rigorous, and evolvable engineering framework for persistent digital systems.

### Vision

A future where persistent digital systems can be engineered with the same rigor as traditional software systems.

### Scope

The project currently focuses on:

- Engineering Principles
- Engineering Methodology
- System Architecture
- Governance
- Technical Specifications
- Reference Implementations

### Non-Goals

The current project does not aim to:

- Define Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)
- Study consciousness or philosophy of mind
- Define legal personhood
- Predict future AI development
- Replace existing software engineering

The exclusion of these topics does not imply that they are unimportant. They are simply outside the current engineering scope of this project.

### Engineering Commitments

- Open Development
- Engineering Rigor
- Versioned Knowledge
- Long-term Maintenance
- Reproducibility

### Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it gradually establishes:

- Canonical engineering documents
- Open engineering specifications
- Reference implementations
- An open collaborative community

### References

- README
- ROADMAP
- GOVERNANCE
- LICENSE

This Charter defines the mission and scope of the Digital Life Engineering project. All future work shall remain consistent with this Charter unless formally revised.

# 中文

## 项目目标

Digital Life Engineering（数字生命工程，DLE）项目旨在建立一个开放的工程基础，用于支持长期连续数字系统的设计、构建、治理与演进。

## 项目使命

建立一套开放、严谨、可持续演进的长期连续数字系统工程框架。

## 项目愿景

让长期连续数字系统能够像传统软件一样进行系统化工程实践。

## 项目范围

当前项目主要关注：

- 工程原则
- 工程方法
- 系统架构
- 工程治理
- 技术规范
- 参考实现

## 非目标

当前项目不直接研究：

- 通用人工智能（AGI）的定义
- 意识及心灵哲学
- 法律人格
- AI 未来发展预测
- 替代传统软件工程

上述内容并非不重要，仅不属于本项目当前工程范围。

## 工程承诺

- 开放开发
- 工程严谨
- 知识版本化
- 长期维护
- 可复现

## 成功标准

当项目逐步建立以下成果时，可认为达到既定目标：

- 权威工程文档体系
- 开放工程规范
- 参考实现
- 开放协作社区
